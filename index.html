Welcome to Ives

Hanna's Island on Animal Crossing New Horizons

Welcome to Ives, my island inside Animal Crossing New Horizons. The island name comes from my cat, Ivy. ❤ Take some time to meet the villagers currently living on the island. The island itself is under construction, but will one day be flourishing!

Sherb is goat villager. When he isn't sleeping he's likely tending to his garden or searching the island for bugs. He is often found wearing a sweater, even in summer.

Pietro is a sheep and the favored villager on Ives. He might be a bit camera shy, but he always sporting a good attitude. He hopes to be an artist one day. So, you'll often find him at home crafting something new.

Anabelle is an anteater (despite looking like a pangolin) with more positivity than most can handle. Her goal is to become famous and look good while doing it! She can often be distracted, but she is a good friend to the other villagers.

Wolfgang is a wolf, making his name all the more fitting. He is a cranky villager, but you wouldn't know it by interacting with him. He isn't a very active villager, often found sitting in calm areas of the island or right outside his home.

Deidre is a deer, as her name suggests. Her favorite place on my island is the butterfly room inside the museum (which deserved so much more than a still image).

Molly is a duck and the smallest of all the villagers. She hopes to be a florist one day so you'll often find her watering various flowers around the island.

Ava is a chicken and one of the oldest residents of Ives. She often spends her time strolling the island or singing. As you might expect, she often plays the role of mother hen.

Norma is a cow villager. Her best friend is Ava, they are often found together on Ives. Their personality is quite similar. Her favorite song is the "Mountain Song."

Muffy, another sheep villager, was much welcomed by everyone on Ives. However, she has recently decided to venture off to other lands. She will be missed dearly!

Yuka, a koala, is Ives' newest villager. While she only moved to Ives a few days ago, she has become fast friends with Anabelle. Despite her snooty personality, she bonds with Anabelle over their shared love of fashion.
